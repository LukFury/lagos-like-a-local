🌊 Beaches

Praia Dona Ana

Golden cliffs, calm water, the postcard shot — about 20 min walk from old town.

Free

Praia do Camilo

Stairs down are closed right now — but the clifftop view over the cove is still unreal on its own.

Free · view only

Praia do Pinhão

No safe way down anymore, but the tiny cove just past Dona Ana is worth a look from the clifftop path.

Free · view only

Praia de Porto de Mós

Open Atlantic side, real waves if you want surf instead of calm water.

Free

Meia Praia

5km of sand, empty compared to the cove beaches — good for a long walk.

Free

🧭 Activities

Lagos Old Town walk

Praça Luís de Camões, the Church of Saint Anthony, cobbled streets — an hour well spent before or after the water.

Free

Mercado Municipal de Lagos

Real working fish and produce market — the rooftop café has a great view of the promenade.

Free

Cabo de São Vicente / Sagres day trip

Westernmost point of mainland Europe, dramatic and worth the half-day.

Free

Bike rental along the coastal path

Cheap, easy way to cover more cliffs and beaches than you could on foot in a single afternoon.

From €10

Ponta da Piedade boardwalk

New ~2km clifftop boardwalk along the sandstone cliffs and sea stacks — a proper walk, not just a photo stop.
